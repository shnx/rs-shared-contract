# Marketing Strategy & Ecosystem Identity

**Date:** July 8, 2026
**Purpose:** Define the brand identity, pricing strategy, and ecosystem approach for the consultation platform.

---

## Core Philosophy

### Ecosystem, Not Funnel

**The Journey:**
```
Curious person → Community member → Student → Builder → Professional → Mentor → ADY collaborator
```

**Key Principle:** Every section of the website answers "What happens to you after working with me?" not "What do I do?"

### Don't Sell Hours. Sell Outcomes.

- **Students** buy career acceleration (avoid months of mistakes)
- **Companies** buy risk reduction (one decision saves €20,000)
- The platform is the digital headquarters of Mohammad Shannak — robotics, AI, automation, startup engineering

### The "Builder" Identity

Replace "student" with "builder" in all communications:
- "Hello Builder" instead of "Hello Student"
- Reinforces identity of creators, not consumers
- Aligns with long-term vision for ADY

## Pricing Structure (Outcome-Based)

| Service | Duration | Price | Target | Deliverables |
|---------|----------|-------|--------|--------------|
| **First Coffee ☕** | 30 min | €15 | Students | Quick advice, no prep, no follow-up |
| **Student Mentoring** | 60-90 min | €60-120 | Students | Prep included, action plan, resources, recording |
| **Technical Consultation** | 60-90 min | €180-300 | Professionals | Architecture review, debugging, product design |
| **Company Advisory** | 90 min | €350-600 | Companies | Technical advisory, feasibility study, strategy |
| **Half-Day Workshop** | 4 hours | €1,000-2,000 | Companies/Startups | Deep dive, hands-on |
| **Monthly Advisor** | Monthly | €1,000-5,000 | Companies | Weekly meetings, Slack/WhatsApp support, reviews |

**Important:** Company sessions include more scope (document review, written recommendations, follow-up) — they're paying for a larger engagement, not just time.

**First Coffee Positioning:** "This isn't just a consultation—it's the first conversation in what could become a long professional relationship." The €15 price is intentionally low to remove barriers for passionate students.

## CRM Lead Scoring System

### 9 Scoring Dimensions (0-100 each)

| Dimension | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Technical** | Demonstrated technical ability through projects, code, CV |
| **Curiosity** | Questions asked, initiative to learn, exploration |
| **Communication** | Writing quality, clarity in responses, interview performance |
| **Consistency** | Regular engagement, follows through on commitments |
| **Initiative** | Starts projects, self-directed, takes action |
| **Portfolio** | Quality and depth of work samples |
| **Availability** | Responsiveness, scheduling flexibility |
| **Leadership** | Mentoring others, leading projects, community contribution |
| **Entrepreneurship** | Startup mindset, business thinking, innovation |

**Overall Score:** Average of all dimensions

**Purpose:** When companies ask for engineers, you already know exactly who to recommend. You become a talent discovery engine.

## Biggest Marketing Mistake to Avoid

**Wrong:** "What do I do?"
**Right:** "What happens to you after working with me?"

Your visitor doesn't care that you're an expert in robotics. They care that after interacting with you, they'll have:
- A clearer career path
- A stronger product
- A better engineering decision
- A faster route to their goal

Every section of the website must be written from that perspective.
